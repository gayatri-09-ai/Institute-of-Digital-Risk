About IDR


The Institute of Digital Risk (IDR) is an industry-led training and deployment institute focused on digital, cyber and technology risk roles in regulated and high-consequence environments.


Through partnerships with leading universities and real industry projects, IDR prepares professionals to manage complex digital risks across sectors including financial services, government and critical infrastructure.
